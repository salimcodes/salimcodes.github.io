Hey. Hi. I am Salim Oyinlola.

Welcome to my website! I am an Undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the University of Lagos.

An IEEE Computer Society Richard E. Merwin Scholar, I am affiliated with the AI and Robotics Laboratory under Dr. Chika Yinka-Banjo and supervised by Prof. Peter Olabisi Oluseyi. My research explores machine learning for intelligent decision-making in energy systems and agriculture.

I believe in the power of storytelling. Through the Beyond the Classroom platform, I document student success to inspire others. I also served as a GitHub Campus Expert and Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador.

Outside of engineering, I champion mental health advocacy through the SoundAsABell Initiative, focusing on SDG 3. Non-work-wise, I follow football, the NBA, and Formula 1, and enjoy reading autobiographies.
