Hey. Hi; Welcome to my website!

I am an Undergrad. student in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the University of Lagos. I am interested in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT), and Cloud Technologies, as well as in applications of technology in energy.

In addition to my academic activities, I am actively involved in tech communities and I constantly share my knowledge and experience in AI, IoT, Cloud technologies - particularly Microsoft Azure, and GitHub for open source. I also serve as a GitHub Campus Expert and Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador in my school. Furthermore, I author technical articles on everything between Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning and Cloud Computing. In that regard, I try to approach technical writing as a story-telling experience.

Also, in line with my passion for making impact, I champion a mental health advocacy group named SoundAsABell Initiative. The initiative, which addresses the third Sustainable Development Goal [Good Health and Well-Being], is geared towards educating young Nigerians about mental illnesses in a bid to alleviate the stigma and stereotypes associated with persons suffering from mental illnesses.

Also, just because I understand the importance of telling stories, I tell stories of students amazing things in a bid to inspire others with Beyond the Classroom SoundAsABell Initiative.

Non-work-wise, I follow football, the NBA, and Formula 1. When I am not binge-watching some random sitcom, I try to add to my bank of "useless" knowledge by reading autobiographies of as many people as I can draw inspiration from. I could also be writing for fun.
